====================================================
Quiz
====================================================

.. note::

   Quiz questions for L1 will be available after the lecture.

Sample Review Questions
-----------------------

1. What is the **Dynamic Driving Task (DDT)** and what does it include/exclude?

2. Explain the difference between **ADAS** (Levels 1--2) and **ADS** (Levels 3--5).
   Who is responsible for the DDT in each case?

3. Define **Operational Design Domain (ODD)**. Give an example of geographic,
   environmental, and traffic-related ODD restrictions.

4. A company deploys a robotaxi service that operates only in downtown Phoenix
   during clear weather and daytime. What SAE level does this most likely
   represent? Justify your answer.

5. Name three major companies deploying Level 4 robotaxi services as of 2026
   and briefly describe their operational status.

6. List the six core technical challenges in autonomous driving discussed in
   this lecture.

7. What is the difference between **ISO 26262** and **ISO 21448 (SOTIF)**?
   Why are both needed?

8. Describe the **client-server architecture** of the CARLA simulator. What
   role does the ROS 2 bridge play?

9. What is the significance of the **UNECE Global Technical Regulation on ADS**
   approved in January 2026?

10. Why does this course use **CARLA 0.9.16** with a custom ROS 2 bridge
    instead of CARLA's native ROS 2 support?
